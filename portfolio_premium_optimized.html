Questions

Before You Reach Out.

The questions every serious client asks first — answered up front.

How long does a project take?+

Fixed-scope projects (landing pages, agents, starter builds) ship in days. Larger web apps, SaaS MVPs, and enterprise platforms are scoped with a clear timeline before work starts — never an open-ended estimate.

Do I own the source code?+

Yes, completely. Full source code, documentation, and credentials are handed over at delivery. There's no lock-in and no dependency on me to keep it running.

Do you provide hosting?+

I set up hosting and deployment (Vercel, AWS, or your preferred provider) as part of delivery. Ongoing hosting costs are billed directly by the provider — I don't mark them up.

Do you offer maintenance?+

Every package includes a post-launch support window. Beyond that, ongoing maintenance and feature work can be arranged as a standing partnership — no fixed retainer required unless you want one.

Can you redesign or rebuild an existing system?+

Yes. Redesigns, migrations, and rebuilding on top of an existing codebase are common — I review what's there first and scope around it rather than starting from a blank slate unnecessarily.

How do payments work?+

Fixed-price packages are split into a deposit and a delivery payment. Custom-quoted work is broken into milestones agreed on during the discovery call, so you're never paying in full upfront for an undefined scope.
